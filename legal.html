Data Collection & Fraud Prevention

SoundsCare collects specific data to ensure platform safety and reduce fraudulent activities.

IP Address Collection

We collect your Internet Protocol (IP) address to:

  • Detect geographic anomalies and suspicious login patterns
  • Prevent account takeover and unauthorized access
  • Identify and block fraudulent registrations
  • Investigate reported disputes and policy violations
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
SECURITY MEASURE

Geolocation Tracking

For employees, we collect:

  • GPS Coordinates: Precise location during registration and work assignments
  • Regional Data: Country and region information
  • Movement Patterns: Travel history between assignments (with consent)

This helps us:

  • Verify you are in an operating region
  • Match you with nearby job opportunities
  • Prevent location-based fraud
  • Ensure safety and compliance

Device Information Collection

We collect device identifiers including:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Device ID and IMEI (if available)
  • Mobile network information

This prevents:

  • Multiple account creation from same device
  • Bot and automated fraud attempts
  • Credential sharing across accounts
  • Spoofed identity registrations
Consent Required: During registration, you explicitly consent to this data collection. You can revoke consent anytime, but this may limit your access to certain features.

Fraud Detection Algorithms

Our system uses machine learning to identify:

  • Suspicious registration patterns
  • Velocity fraud (multiple registrations from same source)
  • Account takeover attempts
  • Payment fraud and chargebacks
  • Fake credential verification

Data Minimization

We collect only the minimum data necessary for fraud prevention. We do not collect:

  • Biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition) without explicit consent
  • Health or medical information
  • Financial account details beyond payment processing
  • Criminal background data (performed only with consent)

Data Access & Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request what data we've collected about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (subject to legal holds)
  • Export your data in portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
