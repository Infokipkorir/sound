Terms & Conditions — Employers

Last updated: | Sounds Care, Nairobi, Kenya

1. Platform Services

Sounds Care connects employers with verified domestic workers in Kenya. We act as an introduction and placement agency only.

2. Employer Access Fee

Employers pay a one-time, non-refundable platform access fee of KES 1,000. This fee grants: full access to verified worker profiles, direct messaging, job posting (up to 5 active posts), and contract generation tools.

This fee is strictly non-refundable once payment is confirmed.

3. Replacement Guarantee

If a placed worker leaves within the first 30 days, Sounds Care will provide one free replacement, subject to written notice within 7 days.

4. Employer Obligations

  • Pay workers agreed salary on time and in full.
  • Comply with the Kenya Employment Act 2007.
  • Treat all domestic workers with dignity and respect.
  • Not share worker contact details with third parties without consent.

5. Liability Limitation

Sounds Care is not liable for any losses or disputes arising from the employment relationship.

6. Governing Law

These terms are governed by Kenyan law. Disputes shall be resolved through mediation first, then the courts of Kenya.
