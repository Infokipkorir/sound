Terms & Conditions — Employers

Last updated: | Sounds Care, Nairobi, Kenya

1. Platform Services

Sounds Care connects employers with verified domestic workers in Kenya. We act as an introduction and placement agency only. The employment contract is solely between the employer and worker.

2. Employer Access Fee

Employers pay a one-time, non-refundable platform access fee of KES 1,000. This fee grants:

  • Full access to verified worker profiles including contact details
  • Direct messaging with workers and admin support
  • Job posting capability (up to 5 active posts per cycle)
  • Contract generation tools and placement assistance
This fee is strictly non-refundable once payment is confirmed, regardless of whether a suitable worker is found or a placement is made.

3. Replacement Guarantee

If a placed worker leaves within the first 30 days, Sounds Care will provide one free replacement, subject to written notice from the employer within 7 days of the worker's departure. No additional placement fee applies for the replacement.

4. Employer Obligations

  • Pay workers agreed salary on time and in full.
  • Comply with the Kenya Employment Act 2007 regarding leave, notice periods, and statutory deductions (NHIF/NSSF).
  • Treat all domestic workers with dignity and respect.
  • Not share worker contact details with third parties without consent.

5. Liability Limitation

Sounds Care is not liable for any losses, damages, or disputes arising from the employment relationship. All workers are background-checked to reasonable standards but we cannot guarantee conduct, character, or performance. Employers are encouraged to conduct their own interviews and trial periods.

6. Governing Law

These terms are governed by Kenyan law. Disputes shall be resolved through mediation first, then the courts of Kenya.
