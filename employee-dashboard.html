Terms & Conditions — Amilia Care

Last updated:  |  Amilia Care, Nairobi, Kenya

1. Services

Amilia Care is a domestic staffing platform that connects employers (families seeking household help) with workers (domestic employees seeking employment) in Kenya. Amilia Care acts as an introduction and placement agency only and is not an employer of the workers listed on its platform.

2. Registration & Eligibility

Users must be 18 years or older. By registering you confirm all information provided is accurate and truthful. False information may result in immediate account suspension without refund.

3. Fees — Workers

Workers are charged the following non-refundable fees:

  • Registration Fee: KES 300 — paid once upon account verification. Covers profile listing, ID verification and background check administration.
  • Placement Fee: KES 1,000 — due upon confirmation of first placement. This is a one-time fee per placement cycle.
All fees paid to Amilia Care are strictly non-refundable, regardless of whether a placement is made, the worker resigns, or the employer terminates employment. No exceptions.

4. Fees — Employers

Employers are charged a one-time platform access fee:

  • Employer Access Fee: KES 1,000 — one-time, non-refundable payment to unlock full contact access to worker profiles and messaging.
This fee is non-refundable once payment is confirmed, regardless of whether a suitable worker is found.

5. Replacement Guarantee

Amilia Care offers a free replacement if a placed worker leaves within the first 30 days of employment, subject to the employer providing written notice within 7 days of the worker's departure. The replacement is provided once at no additional placement fee to the employer. Worker registration fees are not transferable or refundable in replacement scenarios.

6. Worker Conduct & Verification

Amilia Care conducts reasonable verification of worker identities (National ID/Passport, selfie verification, and reference checks where available). However, Amilia Care does not guarantee the conduct, performance, or character of any worker. Employers are encouraged to conduct their own interviews and probationary periods.

7. Employment Relationship

The employment contract is solely between the employer and the worker. Amilia Care is not liable for any disputes, claims, damages or losses arising from the employment relationship including but not limited to theft, misconduct, injury, or termination disputes.

8. Privacy

Personal information collected is used solely for platform operations, worker verification, and matching services. Amilia Care will not sell personal data to third parties. Workers' contact details are only shared with verified employers upon confirmed placement.

9. Account Termination

Amilia Care reserves the right to suspend or terminate any account found to be abusive, fraudulent, or in violation of these terms, without refund of any fees paid.

10. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Kenya. Any disputes shall be resolved through mediation or the courts of Kenya.
