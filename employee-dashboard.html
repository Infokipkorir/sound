1. Getting Started

Welcome to WorkLink — your professional platform for finding household and support employment opportunities. After logging in, your dashboard gives you a complete view of your job search, interview messages, payments, and profile.

2. Completing Your Profile

A complete profile dramatically increases your chances of being contacted by employers. The Profile Completion card on your Overview shows what's missing. Click your avatar or the Profile button to fill in:

  • Full name, phone, date of birth, gender
  • Preferred role, current town, county
  • About yourself, languages spoken, key skills
  • Profile photo (recommended)

3. How Interview Messages Work

Employers review your profile and send interview invitations directly to you. You do not initiate contact — wait for an employer to message you. When one does:

  • A notification appears in your bell icon
  • The Messages → Interview Messages tab shows the conversation
  • You can reply to employer messages in the thread
  • Interviews appear under Ongoing or Complete once processed

4. Applying for Jobs

Go to the Jobs tab, browse or search for openings, and click Apply. Track your applications status (Applied, Shortlisted, Hired, or Rejected) in the My Applications section. Admin may also suggest specific jobs that match your profile.

5. Hired Toggle — What It Does

When you get a job, flip the Hired toggle on your Overview. This:

  • Marks your profile as unavailable to employers
  • Prevents new interview invitations from being sent
  • Shows a green "Hired" badge on your profile
  • Displays an animated banner on your dashboard

Toggle it back off if you become available for work again.

6. Payments Guide

WorkLink charges two fees: a KES 300 Registration Fee (due before your profile goes live) and a KES 1,000 Placement Fee (due on your first salary). Payments are made via M-Pesa or Airtel Money STK Push. All fees are strictly non-refundable.

7. OTP Verification & Badge

Verify your phone number via OTP to earn a Verified badge on your profile. Verified employees are prioritized in employer searches and get 3× more interview requests. Go to Quick Actions → Verify & Get Badge to start.

8. Account Settings

Under Settings you can: sign out, change notifications preferences, deactivate your account (temporary pause), or permanently delete your account. Deactivation hides your profile; deletion is permanent.

9. FAQ

Your phone is visible only after you accept an interview request. Until then, only your profile details are shown to employers.
Our admin team typically reviews profiles within 24–48 hours on business days. You'll receive a notification when your status changes.
Your profile will remain in draft mode until the registration fee is paid. You can still browse jobs and apply, but employers won't be able to contact you.
When you enable the Hired toggle, your profile is marked as unavailable. Employers searching for staff will not be shown your profile, and you won't receive new interview requests until you toggle it off.

A non-refundable registration fee of KES 300 is required before your profile is made visible to employers. A placement fee of KES 1,000 is charged on your first salary upon successful placement. All fees paid are strictly non-refundable.

You are responsible for ensuring your profile information is accurate and up-to-date. Providing false information may result in immediate account termination without refund.

3. Employment Relationship

WorkLink acts solely as an introduction platform. We are not party to any employment contract between you and an employer. All employment disputes are between the employee and employer.

4. Account Conduct

You agree not to misuse the platform, contact employers outside the platform without consent, or engage in any fraudulent activity. Violations may result in permanent account deletion.

5. Privacy

We collect and process personal data in accordance with Kenyan Data Protection laws. Your information is used solely for matching you with suitable employment opportunities.

WorkLink reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
